# Navigating the Landscape of Multimodal AI in Medicine: A Scoping Review

**Source**: Medical Image Analysis (ScienceDirect, May 2025)
**URL**: https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1361841525001689
**Access**: Paywalled (Elsevier) - summary from abstract and search results
**Relevance**: Large-scale scoping review (432 papers, 2018-2024) with quantitative performance analysis

---

## Overview

Scoping review examining 432 papers on deep learning-based multimodal AI across the medical domain (2018-2024).

## Key Quantitative Finding
Multimodal AI models consistently outperform unimodal counterparts with an **average improvement of 6.2 percentage points in AUC**.

## Persistent Challenges Identified
- Cross-departmental coordination for data collection
- Heterogeneous data characteristics across modalities
- Incomplete datasets (missing modalities)
- Varying data quality across institutions

## Scale of Review
- 432 papers analyzed
- Covers 2018-2024 timeframe
- Descriptive analysis across all medical domains

## Key Takeaways for Our Project
- 6.2% AUC improvement is the expected multimodal gain to benchmark against
- Missing modality handling is consistently identified as a top challenge
- Cross-institutional data heterogeneity must be addressed for generalization
