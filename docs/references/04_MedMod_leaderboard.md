## Overview

MedMod is a benchmarking platform evaluating unimodal and multimodal clinical models on MIMIC-IV and MIMIC-CXR datasets across six clinical prediction tasks.

## Benchmark Tasks
1. In-hospital mortality prediction
2. Patient decompensation forecasting
3. Length of stay estimation
4. Hospital readmission risk assessment
5. Clinical condition prediction
6. Radiological finding identification

## Model Categories

### Supervised Learning (6 models)
Three fundamental fusion paradigms tested:
- Early fusion integration
- Joint fusion mechanisms
- Late fusion strategies
Plus additional sophisticated multimodal frameworks.

### Self-Supervised Learning (3 methods)
State-of-the-art self-supervised techniques assessed alongside supervised models.

## Evaluation Methodology
Unified evaluation scheme and protocol for each task, enabling consistent cross-model comparison.

## Key Takeaways for Our Project
- Standard benchmark tasks we should align with
- Comparison against established baselines is expected
- Both supervised and self-supervised approaches should be considered
